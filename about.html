About Milano

Our story and passion for authentic Italian pizza

Milano Restaurant was founded in 2010 with one mission: to serve authentic Italian pizza with unparalleled quality and taste. Inspired by Neapolitan traditions, we are committed to using the best ingredients and traditional preparation methods.

Our pizzas are passionately crafted by our expert chefs who bring decades of experience from Italy. We import our flour, tomatoes, and cheese directly from Italy to ensure the most authentic flavors.

Each pizza is cooked in our traditional wood-fired oven at 450°C, giving it that perfect crispy crust and smoky flavor that characterizes true Neapolitan pizza.

At Milano, we believe that great pizza starts with great ingredients and ends with a satisfied customer. Discover the taste of Italy in the heart of the city.

We take pride in being the go-to destination for pizza lovers in the area. Our team is dedicated to providing an exceptional dining experience to every guest, with premium quality, friendly service, and a warm atmosphere.
